<h2>Corporate announcements</h2>
<p>
  Ready-to-edit templates are available for <b>internal email communications targeted to WFP employees</b>:
</p> 
<p>
  <ul>
    <li><strong>Pure text announcement</strong>, suitable for narrative, short announcements, with no or just one link in the body, e.g. senior staff announcements.</li>
    <li><strong>Text announcement with highlight box</strong>, suitable to emphasize a piece of information in the body of the message, e.g. WFP directive announcement.</li>
    <li><strong>Announcement with image, call-to-action link and teaser</strong>, suitable to promote products or services, to foster participation in campaigns, or whenever you want your audience to take an action.</li>
  </ul>
  <br/>
  The templates are available as Outlook email templates, for both Windows and Mac, and rely on a simple, usable layout with minimal and effective branding. They work in Microsoft Outlook, Microsoft Outlook on the Web and most other email clients. <br/>
  <br/><a href="https://newgo.wfp.org/documents/email-templates-for-announcement">Download the template files and the the guidance (access restricted to WFP employees)</a>.
</p>

<h2>System notifications</h2>
<p>
  A template for <b>automated notifications issued from corporate systems</b> is also available in an HTML format: it is useful to inform selected users about specific activities in the system.<br />
  <br />Examples of <b>use cases</b> are:<br/> ​
  <ul>
    <li>A new record is added in the system and assigned a unique identifier – users need to be notified about the successful registration.​</li>
    <li>The status of a record is updated – users need to be notified about the changes and progress in the workflow.</li>
    <li>An action is needed – users need to take action for the workflow to be completed, e.g. approve, reject, request more info​.</li>
  </ul>
  <br />To use the template, please contact <a href="mailto:global.uikit@wfp.org">global.uikit@wfp.org</a>.
</p>
